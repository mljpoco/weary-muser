About

Share This Page

Created initially as a means of breaking into front-end software development, this blog quickly morphed into a passion project. I love writing, and I loved the idea that I could present mine to the world in a manner that'd be exclusively to my taste. No worrying about stylistic constraints, (literary or other) editors, invoices, or expectations generally. This is my altar — over which I am entirely free to bleed.

Whether that seems dramatic to you is far from being my business. To you, reader, my responsibilities are as follows: Keep the entries flowing, and ensure their quality is such that I, the author, can stand to read each one repeatedly — even after they've been posted longer than a few weeks, months, or years. I think it safe to reckon any self respecting writer would conscionably admit that the height of such a standard is nothing to sneeze at. It is, in fact, perpetually just out of reach — which is why I peruse the entire body of work to make edits on a regular basis.

As far as what's to be had in terms of content — all sorts can be found here. In addition to regular vacillation between the measured and the poetic, you may sometimes come across an entry that's blatant in its adherence to the conventions natural to whichever flavor of writing took me on the day(s) it was written. Typically, though, traditional contraints are far from my awareness while I'm working.

If there is any miniscule item of untold information your curiosity binds you to sniff out as it pertains to this blog or myself after having read this, I implore you to visit my contact page, kindly introduce yourself — and present your query.
